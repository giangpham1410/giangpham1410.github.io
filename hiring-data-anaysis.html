Can talent assessment scores during the hiring process for Deputy Managers (DMs) be linked to future job performance?

Skills: Pivot tables, data visualization

1. Dataset Summary:

The dataset includes about 40 management trainees from MBA colleges. These employees were assessed on competencies at the point of recruitment through Talent Assessments. HR wants to link Quantitative Assessment outputs with Performance results.

Data Dictionary

2. Dimensions & Measures

  • Dimensions: Employee ID, Location.
  • Measures: Performance_score, Achieving Goals & Objectives, Team Player Qualities, Meeting Expectations, Value Systems & Adherence, Workplace Management, Managing & Organising, Enterprising Qualities, Analysis & Interpretation, People management, Inductive, Deductive, Abductive, Verbal.

3. Tool: Excel

4. Data fields

  • Columns: 16.
  • Rows: 40.

5. Data analysis

a. Define problem: Company wants to analyze if Talent Assessment Scores during Hiring process for Deputy Managers (DMs) can be linked to future performance on job.

b. Collect: Refer sheet "Raw_Data".

c. Organize: Refer sheet "Data_Processing".

  • Problem: Y variable: performance_score.
  • Missing value: no variable has missing values.
  • Outlier: Y variable is set up based on data validation from 1 to 5; no outlier check is required.
  • Dummy variable: none.
  • Derived variable: Create 2 new columns: Total Score, Average Score.

d. Visualize:

  • Ref sheet "Visualize".
  • Based on the charts, we can find out any trends between Total Score and Performance score.

e. Analyze: Ref sheet "Correlation".

f. Insight:

  • Higher the better scores for:
    • Managing & organising.
    • Enterprising qualities.
    • People management.
    • Team player qualities.
  • Lower the better scores for:
    • Achieving Goals & Objectives.
    • Inductive.
